Executive Summary

report_problem The Problem

Traditional e-commerce templates often suffer from slow page loads and rigid checkout flows, leading to cart abandonment. The client needed a highly customizable, SEO-optimized solution that integrated deeply with existing ERP systems.

check_circle The Solution

Leveraging Next.js for server-side rendering and a headless CMS for content management allowed for sub-second page loads. We implemented a custom-built cart logic and a robust API gateway to handle high-traffic peaks.
